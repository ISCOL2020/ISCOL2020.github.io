The 2020 Israel Seminar on Computational Linguistics

The Annual Meeting of the Israeli Seminar on Computational Linguistics, ISCOL 2020, will be held on Tuesday, September 8, in the Microsoft Reactor Dubnov St 7, Tel Aviv

Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing are active research and development fields in Israel today, both in academia and industry. ISCOL is a venue for exchanging ideas, reporting on work in progress and established results, forming collaborations, and advancing connections between academia and industry. ISCOL is also a friendly stage for students for their first appearance in this community.

Follow @ISCOL2020

Call for papers

We invite presentations on recent work in all areas of computational linguistics, natural language processing, and closely related fields. We accept work underway or published provided that it represents recent and original work of interest to our audience.

Submission is now open

Submission should be a 2-page extended abstract, longer submissions will be accpted. ACL format is advised but any format will be accepted.

ISCOL is a non archival conference, so feel free to submit your papers to other venues.

Submission deadline: August 7
